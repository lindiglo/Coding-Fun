Welcome to The Social Capital

What is The Social Capital?

We are a performing arts bar in the heart of the Danforth Village. We host and teach improv comedy, standup and sketch. We have a great sound system for bands of all sizes and a beautiful cabaret space for smaller combos. We host poetry performances and writing workshops. We have a lovely bar and lounge with drink specials every evening of the week. We also rent rehearsal studios and our performance spaces by the hour, day or longer.

Interested in having your show on one of our stages? Want to book a private event or party? Have any other questions? Please get in touch.

Where is The Social Capital?

We’re located at 154 Danforth Avenue, just a few steps from Broadview subway station. We’re on the second and third floor above the venerable Black Swan Tavern.
